Report – Purpose & Scope

Purpose & Objectives

  • Accountability, learning, and decision support across the HDP nexus.
  • Inform next CPD; current CP extended through Dec 2026.

Scope

  • Temporal: 2022–mid-2025, forward-looking to 2026.
  • Geographic: National + priority districts + Cox’s Bazar.
  • Thematic: SRHR; GEWE/GBV; Adolescents & Youth; Population Dynamics. Cross-cutting: LNOB, disability inclusion, innovation, HDP nexus.

Approach & Methods

  • Theory-based, contribution analysis; four thematic ToCs.
  • Participatory, rights-based, LNOB lens; mixed methods; triangulation.
  • Ethics and safeguards for vulnerable groups; informed consent.
